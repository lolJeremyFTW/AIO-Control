---

## Free -- EUR 0

- 1 workspace
- 3 businesses
- Manual plus webhook triggers (no cron quota)
- OpenClaw plus Hermes via your own VPS
- No routine quota
- Community support

## Pro -- EUR 29 / month

- Unlimited businesses
- Cron schedules (on subscription OR API key)
- Telegram plus email notifications
- Spend limits plus auto-pause
- Mobile push (web plus Capacitor)
- **10,000 runs / month included**, then EUR 0.003 per run
- Email support

## Team -- EUR 99 / month

- Everything from Pro
- Unlimited members plus role-based access
- Per-business isolated mode (no workspace fallback)
- Audit log export plus GDPR DSR helpers
- **100,000 runs / month included**, then EUR 0.002 per run
- Priority support (24h response)

## Yearly billing

10x monthly price for 12 months = 2 months free. Pro EUR 290/year, Team EUR 990/year.

## Switching plans

Settings > Subscription > Change plan. Pro-rated billing via Stripe. Downgrade takes effect on the next billing cycle.
