Three blocks are present on every page: the rail (left), the header (top) and the chat panel (bottom right).

## The rail

Always visible on the left. Content adapts to where you are.

### At workspace level

- List of your businesses, clickable, with badge for open queue items
- Workspace actions: Cost, Activity, Runs, Queue, Marketplace, Skills, Agents, Self-Improving, Flows
- At the bottom: Settings, Profile, Theme toggle, Sign out

### Inside a business

The rail switches to:

- "Back" button at the top
- Topic tree of that business
- "+ New topic" button at the bottom

Right-click on a business or topic gives a context menu with options: edit, archive, duplicate, move, change icon, change appearance, copy link.

## The header

At the top:

- **WorkspaceSwitcher** (left) -- quickly switch between workspaces
- **Search button** -- opens a command palette with fuzzy search across everything (workspaces, businesses, agents, runs)
- **Weather chip** -- current city and temperature (configurable in [Settings > Weather](settings))
- **Talk module** -- microphone button with dropdown for agent selection, see [Talk](talk)
- **Notifications bell** -- live count of open queue items and failed runs
- **Theme toggle** -- light or dark
- **Profile letter** -- click for Profile or Sign out

## The floating chat panel

Bottom right you see a green bell. Click to open the chat. Works on every page within the workspace and keeps context across tabs. See [Chat](chat) for details.

## The runs toaster

Bottom right small notifications appear when a run finishes, fails, or lands in HITL. They disappear automatically after 8 seconds or on dismiss.

## Shortcuts

| Key | What |
|-------|-----|
| `Ctrl+K` | Open search from any page |
| `Esc` | Close chat panel or dialog |
| `/` | Open command palette in chat |
| `Enter` | Send message |
| `Shift+Enter` | New line in message |
